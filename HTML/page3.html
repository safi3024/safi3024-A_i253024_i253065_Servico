About us

Servico was founded on the simple belief that connecting local expertise with local need shouldn't be complicated.

Based right here in Pakistan, our mission is to redefine the home and workplace service industry by building a trustworthy, transparent, and efficient marketplace.

We understand the challenges faced by both customers in finding verified, reliable professionals for tasks like plumbing, electrical work, and construction, and by skilled laborers in securing consistent, local job opportunities.

Our unique, smart location technology is at the heart of our platform, ensuring customers are instantly matched with the best nearby service providers, fostering faster response times and strengthening community commerce.

We are committed to uplifting local skill, guaranteeing quality service, and ensuring every interaction on the Servico platform is built on safety, fairness, and mutual trust.
