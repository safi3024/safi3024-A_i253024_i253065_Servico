FAQ

Section 1: For Customers (Hiring Services)

Q: How do I book a service on Servico?

A: Search for the service you need, select your location, review provider profiles, add the service to your request basket, and confirm the booking details.

Q: Are the service providers verified?

A: Yes, all service providers undergo a strict background check, skill verification, and are continuously rated by our community to ensure quality and trust.

Q: How does the pricing work?

A: Pricing is transparent. It may be a fixed rate for standard tasks, an hourly rate, or based on a custom quote provided after a job assessment.

Q: What is your Service Guarantee if I'm not satisfied?

A: Our guarantee assures you of quality. We have a dedicated process for re-dos, issue resolution, or refunds if a job is not completed to your satisfaction.

Q: Can I track my service provider's arrival?

A: Yes, our platform allows you to track your provider once they have accepted the job and are traveling to your location, thanks to our smart location system.

Section 2: For Service Providers / Workers (Joining the Platform)

Q: How do I sign up to become a service provider?

A: Register via the "For Workers" link in the header or footer, complete the application, pass our necessary background checks, and submit proof of your skills and identification.

Q: How does Servico match me with jobs?

A: Our smart system connects you with jobs based on your skills, availability, and proximity to the customer, prioritizing local opportunities.

Q: How and when do I get paid?

Q: What commission does Servico charge?

A: We charge a transparent fee (either a percentage or fixed rate) on the job's total price to cover platform usage, marketing, and payment processing.

Q: How do customer ratings affect my profile?

A: Customer ratings are vital. Higher ratings lead to improved visibility, more job opportunities, and greater customer trust in your service.
